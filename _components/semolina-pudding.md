---
layout: recipe
title: "Semolina Pudding"

yield: 4 servings

ingredients:
  - 1l milk
  - 250g semolina
  - 50g sugar
  - a pinch of lemon zest
  - a pinch of salt

directions:
  - Combine all ingredients in a pot. Place on the stove and bring to a boil under constant stirring.
